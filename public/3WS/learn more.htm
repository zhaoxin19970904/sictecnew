ASSEMBLY
Follwing the components list, take out the 3-wheel sliding vehicle from the carton box,and make sure all the components are right.
 
     
 
 
STEP 1:
Press down the position nut, and plug the T-Bar into the front fork unit to where the position you want to be.(watch out not to clamp your hand)		   STEP 2-1:
Using customized tool tight up the T-Bar set to mainframe where the position tight and not moved.(Model:0800 Model:1000)
 
     
 
 
STEP 2-2:
Adjust the quick release to where the position tight and not moved.(Model:1200)		   STEP 3:
See the diagram, put the both handle bar into the T-Bar where the position nuts set to the right position

» After assembling the 3-wheel sliding vehicle,First of all,check the left and right brake working and sensitive enough.
