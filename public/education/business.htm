SICTEC provides training programs of business administration in the North America and Europe to Chinese corporate managers from large state-owned enterprises and business associations. These programs, delivered by renowned professors in schools of business administration and executives from western enterprises, highlight new concept and trends in corporate management in the west with selected case studies. The site tour of selected corporations and facilities in these training programs further enhance the understanding of the management philosophy of western corporations including corporate culture and management system implemented.

The topics of business administration include:

  • Strategic Planning and Corporate Restructure
  • Quality Management System
  • Enterprise Resource Planning
  • Financial Planning and Budget Management
  • Corporate Internal Audit
  • Corporate Human Resource Management
