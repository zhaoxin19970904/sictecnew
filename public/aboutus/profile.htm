  SICTEC Group Inc. is located in Toronto, Canada in the heart of North America. It has been engaged in international development and cooperation, management consulting and international trade since its inception over a decade ago. It has a branch office in China and strategic partners in North America, South America, Europe and Australia. Its subsidiary, Sino-Canada Technology Exchange Center is accredited by the Chinese government as its legal agent to provide executive training programs to senior Chinese government officials and corporate managers overseas.

SICTEC, in partnership with various government agencies, professional associations and companies in the west, develops projects of technological and economic cooperation in China. It also contributes to economic development in China through the introduction of western technologies and management expertise, as well as capital investment. SICTEC provides consulting services to Chinese companies to assist their international market development efforts in the areas of strategic partnerships, marketing strategies and distribution network development. Through partnerships with renowned institutions and government agencies, SICTEC also provides career education and management training programs in China.
