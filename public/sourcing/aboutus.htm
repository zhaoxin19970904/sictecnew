About Us

SICTEC Sourcing Solutions is an integrated business services provider that helps companies in North America and Europe reduce their operating and sourcing costs by enabling access to sources of high quality, lower cost products from China.

Our sourcing services are supported by 12 years of business operations and activity in China, and by our extensive network of contacts in both the Chinese business community and in government agencies.

SICTEC sourcing managers work closely with our clients to define successful sourcing projects.

Our SICTEC Beijing office then executes a thorough market/industry study to identify suppliers that meet pre-determined qualifications. We conduct extensive investigations of their management, history, affiliations, product lines, quality management practices, exporting expertise and capitalization.

With SICTEC's office and professional sourcing team in China, we provide real business opportunities, tangible cost savings and measurable results to our clients.

Whether your needs are for a single product, for a line of products, or to set up a joint venture partnership to manufacture products, SICTEC Sourcing Solutions is your best alternative.
