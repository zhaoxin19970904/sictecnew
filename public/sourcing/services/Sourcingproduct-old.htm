Sourcing products
Once the project has been well defined, the SICTEC sourcing team will select the reputable suppliers and manufacturers in China through in-situ due diligence study, and communicate the product specification and design objectives with the suppliers and manufacturers in order to secure the competitive bids from the Chinese suppliers and manufacturers. The SICTEC sourcing team will also conduct its background investigation of the suppliers and manufacturers such as their quality management system, export history and reputation and submit along with the bid information to its client.
