　 OUR SERVICES >> Defining project
Defining the Project is the first phase of our sourcing activities. In this phase,a SICTEC sourcing manager works closely with our client to review and analyze the client's sourcing requirements and to define the scope and objectives of the project.

This includes subjects such as:
» Sales and profit potential
» Product specifications and performance
» Regulatory compliance
» Packaging, labelling and documentation requirements
» Import and export requirements
» Manufacturer selection criteria
» Price/Cost targets
» Approval process
» Commercial Agreement objectives
» Timeframe

These steps ensure that the SCITEC sourcing manager understands the full requirements of the client's sourcing Project.
