The rapid growth of the Chinese economy and improvements in the domestic business environment have made China a favored country for foreign investment. China has become the world manufacture source and is a focus market for overseas businesses in the finance, services and high-tech sectors.

Using its strong linkage to foreign governments, trade organizations and companies, SICTEC Group Inc. help the provincial and municipal governments in China to attract the foreign investment in the development of regional economies. SICTEC also provides services to the Chinese governments and companies in their overseas missions for trade and economic cooperation.
