SICTEC Group Inc. has been engaged for many years in the development of bilateral business and cooperative relationships between the Chinese governments, institutions and business, and their counterparts overseas. SICTEC's dedicated team of senior advisors, who together bring many years of management experiences in the public sector in the west, and specialists that encompass a broad range of expertise have ensured "win-win" cooperation and long lasting relationships among partners.

The SICTEC areas of economic and trade cooperation include:

  • Planning and development of the regional economies and industrial clusters
  • Sustainable agriculture development
  • Rural and small town planning
  • Land use planning and development
  • Livestock production technology and management
  • Urban eco-environment management
  • Technology commercialization and technology transfer
  • Community education and career education
  • E-government and public information access
