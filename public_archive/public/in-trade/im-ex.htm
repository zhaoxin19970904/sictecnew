The SICTEC trade division imports high quality retail and industrial products and distributes them in North America. It also represents a number of Chinese manufacturers and distributes their lines of products in North America, providing one-stop services in marketing strategy, sales, distribution network and services for these products. The trade division has a highly professional team including experienced marketing and sales managers as well as packaging designers.

The trade division also markets advanced western technologies, products and services in China. Staff in SICTEC's Beijing office work closely with its North America headquarters to develop strategic alliance with the distributors and user groups in China to promote the use of these technologies, products and services.
