ABOUT US
 
SICTEC Sourcing Solutions is a 12-year-old company with offices in Beijing, China and Toronto, Canada. We are experts in finding and evaluating manufacturers in China on behalf of our clients in North America and Europe.

We are not consultants. We provide practical, functional solutions with real companies, real products, and real sales.

Our staff in Beijing research domestic markets to identify the highest quality and lowest cost producers and conduct extensive investigations of their management, history, affiliations, product lines, quality management practices, R&D, exporting expertise and capitalization.

Whether your needs are for a single product, for a line of products, or to set up a JV partnership to manufacture products, SICTEC Sourcing Solutions is your best alternative.
