  As China joins the WTO and becomes integrated into the global economy, there is a growing need for both the public and private sectors in China to restructure their management systems and industrial sectors. Partnership with foreign governments and business communities is a key approach in China's reform process, especially when it comes to infrastructure development and managerial innovation. The SICTEC Group Inc. and its business associates have been working with China for over ten years and continues to play an important role in developing bilateral business relationships with Chinese government agencies, as well as the country's public and private institutions. SICTEC believes that these strategic alliances are of mutual benefit to both Chinese institutions and their counterparts abroad.
