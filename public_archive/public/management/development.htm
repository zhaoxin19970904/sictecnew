  SICTEC provides services to Chinese companies that plan to develop their international market in the areas of market development strategy, marketing and sales support, distribution channel development, strategic alliance, after-sale support. It also distributes a number of Chinese brand products and represents manufacturers in attending trade shows and conducting agreement negotiation.

SICTEC works with foreign comparies in their entry to the Chinese market, providing consulting services to assist their market development in China.

Each project is led by a senior manager and a project team that consist of a project consultant, project manager and assistants.
