Today, career education has become an important part of the education system. Countries in the west have developed successful career education system that links closely the curriculum to industrial needs and delivered through both the public and private institutions.

SICTEC, in partnership with the government agencies and education institutions in the west, promotes western career education system in China. Its modular curriculum design follows market demand and the applications of high technologies such as multi-media and distant education tools ensure to best use the vast education resources and database in the west. It can help to train the industrial specialists and skilled workers in a relatively short period of time.
