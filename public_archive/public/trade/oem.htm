More and more western manufacturers are moving their production lines to China as "globalization" becomes irreversible. SICTEC's sourcing team assists companies in North America and Europe in establishing the manufacturing of OEM products in China, and conducts due diligence studies regarding markets, partner qualifications, and project feasibility, and assists its clients to develop strategies and business plans to manufacture the OEM products in China.
