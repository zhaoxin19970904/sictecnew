George Xu (BSc, MSc, PhD) has worked with the Canadian federal government and as a consultant for Canadian government agencies and high-tech companies on developing bilateral projects and international relationships with China. His experience working with technology and China-related business led him to found the Sino-Canada Technology Exchange Centre to which he brings a wealth of knowledge in both public and private sectors.

Jeff Watson (BA, MA) has been working with China for over ten years. After coordinating academic research on Asia and managing Executive Business Programs for two of Canada's largest Universities, he has assumed the role of Director of Programs for the Sino-Canada Technology Exchange Centre.

Laurie Wu (BSc, P.Eng) is the China Division Director at the Sino-Canada Technology Exchange Centre. She manages all client relationships as well as the logistics support program at SICTEC.
