Negotiating agreements


Once our client has tested and approved the products of the selected manufacturers, the SICTEC sourcing team begins the negotiation of final purchasing terms and agreements.

The negotiations include, but are not limited to:
» Prices and price incentives
» Quality standards
» Specifications
» Minimum order requirements
» Production and delivery lead-times
» Quality audit procedures
» Packaging and labelling requirements
» Certification standards (UL, ASTM, CSA etc)
» Payment terms
» Financial transaction systems and procedures
» Product warranty terms
» Claims procedures
» Distribution Agreements
» Other items as required

Upon request, SICTEC can make recommendations for legal firms in China to act on behalf of our clients.
